Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you order.

What is a perfume decant?

A perfume decant is a smaller quantity of fragrance transferred from an authentic original bottle into a travel-sized atomizer.

Are your perfumes authentic?

Yes. Every decant is taken directly from authentic original bottles. We never dilute, mix, or alter the fragrance.

Why buy a decant instead of a full bottle?

Decants let you experience premium fragrances at a lower cost before investing in a full-size bottle.

What sizes do you offer?

We offer 3ml, 5ml, 10ml, 15ml, and selected fragrances in 30ml.

How are decants measured?

We use sterile syringes to ensure accurate volume and maintain the fragrance's quality.

How long does delivery take?

Orders are typically delivered within 1–2 business days in Dhaka and 2–3 business days outside Dhaka.

Can I return or exchange my order?

Opened or used decants cannot be returned. If you receive a damaged, missing, or incorrect item, contact us within 24 hours with a continuous unboxing video.

Where can I collect my order?

Self-pickup is available from Aftabnagar, Banasree, and NSU by prior arrangement.

How can I contact you?

The fastest way is through WhatsApp. You can also reach us via Messenger or Facebook.
