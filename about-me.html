The Story Behind the Decants

Hi, I'm Nawmee —
a fragrance addict turned decant dealer.

I'm Arman Hossain Nawmee — a CSE undergraduate at North South University and, more importantly, someone who accidentally fell headfirst into the world of fragrances in 2024 and never looked back.

"It started with a bottle of Nautica Voyage. One spritz and I was completely hooked. I didn't expect it — I just picked it up randomly. But that fresh, clean scent hit different, and suddenly I needed to know everything about fragrances."

From there I grabbed two decants — JPG Le Male Le Parfum and Kenzo Homme EDT Intense. The Kenzo? Not for me at all. But instead of just letting it sit, I bundled it with Lattafa Khamrah Qahwa and offered it as a combo. It sold instantly. That moment sparked an idea: what if I could share my collection with other fragrance lovers at honest prices?

That's exactly what NawmeEssences is. I decant from my personal 80+ bottle collection using a syringe for precision — no adulteration, no fakes, no nonsense. Just the real thing in the size you actually need, so you can explore, layer, and find your signature scent without spending a fortune on a full bottle.
